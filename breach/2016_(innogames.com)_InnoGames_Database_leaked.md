# InnoGames Database Leaked

------------
**Breach Date:** 2016-06-01

**Domain:** innogames.com

**Leaked Count:** 652276

**Leaked Info:** realname,email,username,password

**File Size:** 52.55 MiB

**File Count:** 1

**File List:** InnoGames.txt

**Download:** https://9ghz.com/breach/InnoGames_Database_375a4f

------------
The InnoGames database leak occurred in 2016 and exposed the personal information of 652,276 users. The leaked data included real names, email addresses, usernames, and passwords. The leak was caused by a vulnerability in the companys website, which allowed unauthorized access to the database. The leaked data was published on a public website, where it could be accessed by anyone. The leak caused InnoGames to reset the passwords of all affected users and to implement additional security measures to prevent future leaks.
